research experience

I start my research experience when I was sofomore. This year I also entered in the cryptography lab.

Math:the preparation for the research

To prepare for my cryptography research, I learned the abstract algebra and the number theory the crypto needs following the direction of professors. And these two courses are all junior level in mathematic major.

What’s more, I enhanced my math skills by learning courses in coursera, Berkeley homepage or other online courses, knowing more about my own major’s math methods.

Start my research in cryptography

In the meantime, I start my research in cryptography. First, I use one year to finish the introduction book of this field: the introduction of modern cryptography。

I also try to follow some master or phd level crypto courses, learning skills and models in graduate students’ research.

My research experience in cryptography

  • In this summer, I took part in the graduate students’ seminar to read papers and discuss cryptography models and some provable security skills.
  • in the seminar , I learn some core models such as public key encryption, identity based encryption and attribute based encryption. What’s more, I also learn some network and information retrieval skills from some application papers and the communication with masters and the phd.

My current research

  1. I start my own research in the domain of fingerprint encryption, which combines pattern recognition and the cryptography. My team will use some new methods to protect users’ 3d fingerprint information, to make it easier to use and more secure.

  2. I take part in 4 programs in the challenge cup and the college student innovation project. Except my fingerprint encryption research, I also take part in the program of network and data processing.
